I am J. Xiong, a Research Postgraduate within the thrust of Smart Manufacturing (SMMG) of the Systems Hub at [The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou)](https://www.hkust-gz.edu.cn/). I am supervised by [Dr. Guo Daqiang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=3auvsZQAAAAJ&hl=en), and my research focuses on human-centric manufacturing, human-robot interaction, human factors and ergonomics, and the human-centric adaptation of robotics. My current work focuses on monitoring and interpreting the physiological states of human operators, with the aim of supporting safer, more efficient, and more adaptive human-robot collaboration.

Please feel free to contact me by email for discussion, collaboration, or further information.
