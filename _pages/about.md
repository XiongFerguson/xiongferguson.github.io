Hello and welcome to my personal website! My name is J. Xiong, and I am currently an MPhil student within the thrust of Smart Manufacturing (SMMG) of the Systems Hub at [The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou)](https://www.hkust-gz.edu.cn/). It is my privilege to be supervised by [Dr. Guo Daqiang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=3auvsZQAAAAJ&hl=en), who guides me in subjects involving Human-Robot Collaboration (HRC), Internet of Things (IoT), and sensors. My research interests include human-centric manufacturing and human-robot collaboration, with a focus on monitoring the physical and physiological conditions of human operators through sensor-based systems.

Please feel free to contact me by email if you have anything to discuss or suggest.
