I am J. Xiong, a Research Postgraduate within the thrust of Smart Manufacturing (SMMG) of the Systems Hub at [The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou)](https://www.hkust-gz.edu.cn/). I am supervised by [Dr. Guo Daqiang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=3auvsZQAAAAJ&hl=en), and my research focuses on human-centric manufacturing, human-robot interaction, human factors and ergonomics, and the human-centric adaptation of robotics. My work aims to support effective and adaptive human-robot collaboration by integrating sensing technologies and intelligent systems for understanding and monitoring the physical and physiological conditions of human operators.

Please feel free to contact me by email for discussion, collaboration, or further information.
